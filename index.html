MY CREATIVE PROCESS




Welcome,

my name is Alyssa and I created this website because I wanted to share ways in which everyone can be a little creative. I wanted to share what inspires me and hopefully helo inspire others aswell. I am currently a senior at Boise State University. I'm studying Interdisciplinary Studies, and I am passionate about graphic design and photography. I also love to draw, paint, and really anything related to art.




About Me

Throughout my time at Boise State I have grown in many different aspects and have learned a lot about myself. Something that really inspired me to make this website about creativity is this TED talk by Sir Ken Robison, which I watched in one of my classes. Robinson talked about how we are all born creative however it is a common conception that creative people are different and were born that way. I found this video very interesting and would highly suggest watching it. Throughout college I have learned that creativity can be heavily dependent on your mindset.
