Everyone can be creative

I often here people caliming to not be creative. A lot of people may think that creativity is just something they are just born with. To an extend I think that's true. Some are naturally more creative than other. However, I know everyone is creative, they just might not realize it. Everyone is creative in their own unique way. There are many way to be creative and it doesn't always have to look the same. When people thing of creative they often automaticly just think of art and artistic people. However, creativity can be seen in all aspects of life.
